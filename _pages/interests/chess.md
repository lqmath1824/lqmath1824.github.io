## My Chess Journey

I started playing chess at the age of 12, learning the fundamentals from classic games and online resources. Over the years, it has become one of my most rewarding intellectual hobbies — a perfect blend of strategy, creativity, and psychological resilience.


> "Chess is not always about winning. Sometimes it's about learning to make the best move in a difficult position." — A lesson that applies equally to mathematics.

## Favorite Resources

- [Lichess](https://lichess.org) — my primary platform for online play and analysis
- *My System* by Aron Nimzowitsch — the classic that shaped my positional understanding
- *Dvoretsky's Endgame Manual* — endlessly challenging
